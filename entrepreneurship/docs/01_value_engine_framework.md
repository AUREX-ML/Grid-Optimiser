# Entrepreneurship Framework — Turning the VPP Startup into a Scalable Venture

## Definitions (from notes)
- **Innovation:** the spark of a new idea or better way; improves or invents solutions to meet unmet needs.
- **Startup:** a temporary, agile organization designed to search for a scalable, repeatable business model.
- **Entrepreneurship:** the mindset/process of taking action; organizing resources, managing risks, and leading a venture from concept to company.

## Combined Result: Disruptive Value
When combined, they produce:
- **Creative destruction:** disrupt incumbents with faster/cheaper/better alternatives.
- **Economic growth:** new companies/products → jobs and development.
- **Market solutions:** turn research/ideas into accessible products for real problems.
- **Scalable success:** innovation = what, entrepreneurship = how, startup = where.
