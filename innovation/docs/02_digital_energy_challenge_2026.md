# Digital Energy Challenge 2026 — Key Dates, Tracks, Grants, Eligibility

## Status
Open for applications as of May 2026.

## Key Dates & Timeline
- Call opened: 20 April 2026
- Information webinar: 5 May 2026
- Application deadline: 17 June 2026 at 23:59 CET
- Selection & bootcamp: Autumn 2026

## Challenge Categories

### Tech Accelerator
- For startups scaling digital energy solutions across 51 African countries (incl. Kenya)
- Grants up to €150,000

### Partnership Challenge
- Collaborative projects in Nigeria with AEDC (Abuja Electricity Distribution Company)
- Grants up to €400,000

## Operational Requirements
- Eligibility: innovative SMEs (<250 employees, annual turnover <€50M)
- Stage: beyond ideation (already piloted) and implementable within 12 months
- Digital nature: software/data/telecom/cloud-based
- Focus areas: grid planning, microgrid/distributed energy management, integrated network visibility
