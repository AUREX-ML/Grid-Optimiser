# OFY Portal vs. Digital Energy Challenge — Clarification

These are distinct entities:
- **Opportunities for Youth (OFY)** is an information hub that lists opportunities.
- **Digital Energy Challenge** is the actual funding program.

## OFY Portal
- Founded: 2009 (some records cite 2010) by Dr. Michael Boampong
- Original mission: bridge the information gap by centralizing scholarships, grants, and professional opportunities
- Evolution: grew from a blog into a non-profit portal managed by Youth Projects World Coast (YPWC)

## Digital Energy Challenge
- Launched: 2020 by Agence Française de Développement (AFD)
- Purpose: part of the Digital Energy Facility (co-financed by EU and ADEME) to address energy access gaps in Africa via digital innovation
- Milestones: first call in 2021; supported 47+ projects (2021–2025)
- 2026: 6th edition partners with Transforming Energy Access (TEA), backed by UK Aid
